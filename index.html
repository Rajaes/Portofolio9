About Me

I am Abdullah Omar, the founder and director of Uratu Creative Agency, specializing in the production of videos and documentaries for global, Arab, and governmental institutions. Throughout my career, I have had the honor of working with prestigious organizations such as TRT, DW, Mosul University, and several Iraqi politicians. With years of experience in the media production field, I established Uratu to provide innovative and high-quality content. I am passionate about creativity and using the latest technologies to ensure the delivery of exceptional media solutions that meet our clients' needs.
